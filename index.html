About Me

Hi there, My name is Ronaldo Pangarego. I am a Data Analyst and currently live in Pontianak, Indonesia. Over the past few months, I've been on a journey of continuous learning, developing my skills in data analysis. It's been an exciting ride, as I've learned to take complex dataset and turn it into clear, actionable insights. I'm also passionate about machine learning. It's a world full of possibilities where data can be used to predict and solve problems.

Within this portfolio, you'll discover a bunch of projects where I've done. Each one is a unique story of how data can be transformed into knowledge. Whether it's understanding market trends, customer behavior, or forecasting the future, I've had the pleasure of simplifying data into meaningful stories.

I'm a learner, a highly organized, self-motivated, creative person, and always like learning new things. Whenever I'm not studying or working, I'm probably watching movies/tv-series or gardening in my spare time.

Languages : Indonesia, English
